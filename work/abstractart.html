Abstract Art Generative Adversarial Network

This is a GAN (Generative Adversarial Network) or neural network I trained to generate artificial images of abstract art at 256x256 resolution. I used my customized version of Nvidia's StyleGAN2-ada-pytorch (available on GitHub) to train on my own curated abstract art data-set.

To gather the initial data-set, I made a script that uses Flickr's API to download images from their database based on a pre-defined keyword. In this case, I used the keyword 'abstract art' when downloading images. This resulted in a directory of over 3,000 images of various art. I then created a script that resizes the images in a given directory to a set (x,y) size. I choose to train on 256x256 sized images because of my machine's overall limited capability (trained on RTX 2080 SUPER).

I only trained this model for around 48 hours, because I was seeing very little improvement to the model in the last 8 hours of training. Results inlcude images that mimic the styles of various visual arts. Although the art itself is extremely abstract, the model did a surprisingly good job of learning and generating different art styles (paintings, pencil drawings, graphitti, etc). Along with some of the generated preview images for this project, I modified StyleGAN2-ada-pytorch to generate video of the artificial images by stitching together multiple images along a particular generated seed's latent vector space.
